Artist Statment

Christmas has always been my favorite day of the year for me.
The regret that the year is coming to an end,
Weak anxiety and anticipation of the beginning of a new year,
Carols passing through the streets,
The dazzling lights of the street and the Christmas decorations,
The clothes of people who change in the face of winter,
White and beautiful snow falling from the sky,
All of these factors made me so excited about Christmas and loved it.



So, as the end of the year is approaching, this project used a blender to make a Christmas cabin.
